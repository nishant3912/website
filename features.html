Features of Alarm Clock

  • Multiple Alarms

    Set multiple alarms to wake up at different times or for different purposes.

  • Customizable Tones

    Choose from a variety of alarm tones to personalize your wake-up experience.

  • Snooze Function

    Tap the snooze button for a few extra minutes of sleep without dismissing the alarm.

  • Brightness Control

    Adjust the brightness of the clock display to your liking, whether it's day or night.

  • 24-hour Format

    Switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats based on your preference.

  • User-friendly Interface

    Enjoy an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for setting up alarms and managing settings.
